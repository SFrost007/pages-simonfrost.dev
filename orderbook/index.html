Order Book

Create orders on the go

Order Book is a sales rep's best friend, allowing customer orders to be taken quickly and easily on the road.

Features

  • Import customer and product data from CSV
  • Deal in any number of currencies
  • Add price overrides, remembered per-customer
  • Send orders by email, export as PDF/CSV, or just save within Order Book

Privacy

I take your and your customers' privacy very seriously, that's why this app contains no user tracking or data harvesting.

I use TelemetryDeck, a privacy-first analytics platform to track general app usage without collecting any of your data or personal identifiers. Examples would be "which settings are enabled" or "how many products are in the database", without capturing any specifics of your data.

Your data stays on your device, full stop.
